Topic: Grocery Billing System
Language: Python

Steps:

Step 1: Read the number of products to be added in the bill.
Step 2: Read name of the product , quantity and price of the product from the user.
Step 3: Calculate and print the total amount of the products.
Step 4: (i)If the customer bought products above 500 rupees, they got discount of 5%.
        (ii)If the customer bought products above 1000 rupees, they got discount of 10%.
        (iii)If the customer bought products above 2000 rupees, they got discount of 15%.
Step 5: The final total amount is printed.
Step 6: Exit
